About ELUM INC

Need something here

ELUM was founded in 2013 as a research and development group focused on designing and bringing to market a new generation of linear, roll-fed, wrap-around labelers. ELUM brought together a group of designers, mechanical engineers, motion control specialists, label and glue experts, and manufacturing professionals with more than 40 years of experience in packaging machinery. This elite group shared a passion for solving problems and building highly efficient machines. Members of the ELUM team hold many patents and possess a wealth of experience in the packaging machinery industry.

Headquartered in Modesto, California, where it manufactures the PROTOS, ELUM draws upon the historical concentration of roll-fed labeler manufacturers in California’s San Joaquin Valley, as well as the electronic and software expertise in nearby Silicon Valley. The company maintains an office and technical support in Querétaro, Mexico.
